# Privacy Policy

**My Register (Register 2.0)**
**Effective Date: April 17, 2026**

## Overview

My Register is designed with your privacy in mind. This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

## Data Collection

My Register does **not** collect any personal information. All data you enter — including register counts, safe counts, business name, and settings — is stored locally on your device using standard app storage.

## Third-Party Services

This app uses **no analytics, advertising, or third-party SDKs**. Your data is never shared with outside companies or services.

## Data Storage

All information remains on your device. My Register does not use iCloud sync, external databases, or network connections of any kind. If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed.

## Children's Privacy

My Register does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be reflected here with an updated effective date.
