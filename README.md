# My Register

Cash register and safe counting app for iOS.

Simplify closing out your registers and safe at the end of each day. Count your bills and coins, and the app calculates your exact deposit — no manual math, no mistakes.

## Features

- Support for up to 10 registers and 1 safe
- Multi-currency support (USD, EUR, GBP, CAD, AUD, JPY, and more)
- Fully customizable denominations
- Setup wizard for quick onboarding
- Localized in 14 languages
- All data stays on your device
